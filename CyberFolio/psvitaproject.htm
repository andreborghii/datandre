New PSVita

Renewing the PSVita is a concept based on the failure of the console launched in 2011 by Sony. The goal is to bring the console back fixing the issues it had and adapting it to the innovations of these years. The design resembles the new PS5 and it adds portable earphones, made for the users to be more into the game even if they're out of their houses.

Console Features

  1. Exclusive Games: exclusive games have always been a factor in the success of game consoles.If the PS Vita had a library of high-quality exclusive games, this could attract the attention of gamers.
  2. Cross-Platform: Having cross-platform play with other consoles such as the PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5 could increase the appeal of the console and allow the same games to be played on both desktop and portable consoles.
  3. Backwards compatibility: It will be possible to try the games that made the old PlayStation generations great, adapted and improved to work best on this portable console.

EarPhones Features

  1. Comfort: Gamers could hear game audio without having to use separate headphones or external speakers.
  2. Quality: Provide better audio quality than speakers built into the console.
  3. Immersive: It allows players to hear all the sounds of the game without having to disturb others around them.
  4. Battery Life: Handle audio more efficiently, thereby increasing console battery life.
