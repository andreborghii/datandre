The Skube

The Skube is a design object that combines the charm of the most famous puzzle in the world with the possibility of hiding objects inside. The design object fits into the post-modern home environment as a piece of furniture. Each face is divided into three/four squares which can be pulled out to reveal hidden compartments, but not all of them can be opened. From aesthetics to functionality, the cyberpunk cube reflects the technological progress.
